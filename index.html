Our Story

Serving the Sacred Indian Cow & Humanity

Mauli Go-Vardhan Goshala, nestled in the serene village of Narivali near Thane, was founded with one sacred purpose — to protect, nurture, and celebrate the divine Indian cow.

We follow ancient Panchgavya traditions to craft products from cow milk, ghee, gomutra, gobar, and curd — five sacred gifts that have healed and nourished Indian families for millennia.

Every product leaving our Goshala is made with reverence, free from chemicals, and charged with the positive energy of healthy, lovingly-tended Desi cows.
